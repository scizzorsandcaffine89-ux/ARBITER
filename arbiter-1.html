PROGRAM OVERVIEW
WHAT IS ARBITER

The Autonomous Review Bureau for Investigation Tribunal Enforcement & Reform was introduced to the public of Tenebris as a neutral oversight mechanism — a system designed to keep courts unbiased, verdicts honest, and investigations clean. That is what it was described as.

What it is, is something else.

Nobody knows who controls ARBITER. Not really. Verdicts shift overnight with no explanation. Entire investigations collapse without a single visible cause. Cases go cold not because they grow stale, but because the foundations beneath them are quietly removed. Evidence vanishes from locked archives. Witnesses relocate to addresses that don't exist. Judges retire early. The machine keeps running.

Some believe ARBITER is a council. Some believe it's a single person. Some believe it's been automated past the point where a person could stop it even if they tried.

Nobody has proven any of this. But everyone in Tenebris can feel it. The weight of something watching. The specific silence that fills a courtroom when a case is about to disappear.

You are now inside the system you were not supposed to find. They know you're here. The question is what you plan to do with the time you have before they decide what to do with you.
