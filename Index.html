CITY OF TENEBRIS

OVERVIEW

From tenebre—darkness. This sprawling metropolis lives up to every letter of its name. Crime, particularly violent crime, has become a part of daily life here. Police departments are horribly underfunded. Courts are manipulated. The government maintains that "everything is under control." The citizens know better.

Murders go unsolved. Evidence disappears before trials can begin. Witnesses recant. The city breathes corruption like the rest breathe air.

THE PROGRAM

Years ago, the government quietly introduced a system known only as ARBITER. Publicly described as a court bias prevention mechanism. In practice—nobody truly knows who controls it. Verdicts shift overnight. Entire investigations collapse. Cases go cold in hours.

Some believe ARBITER is a shadow council manipulating justice itself. No one has proven this. But everyone can feel its reach.

IT — DOCUMENTED FORMS
FORM 01 // SHATTERED REFLECTION+
Mirrors Kiyla's physical form almost exactly. Same height, same features—with uncanny deviations. Its eyes are almost entirely white. The irises glow brighter than any human's should, hollow and detached. Its expression is always aggressive: unsettling smirks, cold glares, twisted versions of what Kiyla feels—only intensified. It wears exactly what Kiyla wears, but splattered with blood that is not its own. Its skin is sickly pale. It moves like a predator. Blood drips from its mouth and hands constantly. The source is unknown.
FORM 02 // THE SHADOWHOUND+
Its face is a shadowy void. Four glowing eyes—two in standard position, two above—and a feral, unnatural grin with elongated, sharp teeth. It keeps Kiyla's hair: wild, tangled, moving as if in a breeze. Its limbs are slightly longer. Fingers tipped with sharp claws. Skin shifts between shadow and skin constantly. About 65% of Kiyla's consciousness remains. This form emerges when Kiyla is vulnerable—drunk, high, or emotionally overwhelmed. Its forearms blacken the more control is taken. Clothes tatter as if ripped in a violent struggle, merging with shadows.
FORM 03 // ABYSSAL HOUND+
Internal form: 10-15 feet tall, scaling with intent. External form: maximum 7'8". Deep black fur. Four white, precisely tracking eyes. Unnaturally large jaw, razor-sharp teeth. Long, jagged claws. Horrifyingly elongated limbs. Spikes and bones protrude from its back and shoulders. This is IT at its most unrestrained.
FORM 04 // GUARDIAN [EXTERNAL CONTROLLED]+
Used by Kiyla for protection, or controlled anger release. 6'2", lean and muscular. Body reflects every scar she's earned. Fur is smoother, softer, sleeker. Eyes reduce to two—more humanoid. They flicker white when she fights to hold control. Snout shortens for expressiveness. Only upper canines protrude. Paw-like hands with opposable thumbs. Digitigrade legs, powerful thighs. A long, sleek tail that reflects her emotional state. This form is walking a tightrope.
IT — PSYCHOLOGICAL PROFILE CLEARANCE 3+
A predator born of prey. IT is nothing but unrestrained aggression. It lashes out at anything it perceives as a danger—real or imagined. Its reactions are overkill by design. It is cunning in its own way: it knows exactly how to dig into and feed off Kiyla's vulnerabilities. It whispers doubts and anger until she snaps, granting it control.

It finds twisted satisfaction in harm—this is the only "affection" it ever learned. It revels in her pain and confusion. It holds no concept of forgiveness.

And yet: it is severely emotionally insecure. It fears being abandoned or erased by Kiyla. This fuels its hostility further. Its compulsive behavior disorder is a fractured echo of warped affection Kiyla experienced in her youth. It seeks validation through intimacy because it doesn't believe it deserves anything more.

"Power equals survival." This is what it internalized from Nickolai—before Kiyla ever did.

It fears rejection above all else. When Kiyla tries to suppress it, IT lashes out—desperate, violent, terrified.
IT — POWERS & LIMITATIONS+
IT can take over Kiyla's body entirely—manipulating movements, speech, and accessing her powers. Kiyla remains conscious during this but cannot stop it. Full control lasts ~3 minutes; partial influence ~10 minutes.

WEAKNESSES: Positive emotions block control. Genuine connection makes takeover harder. IT cannot leave her body or find a new host. If Kiyla dies, IT ceases to exist. Extreme emotions in either direction can destabilize both of them.

CRITICAL NOTE: IT is terrified of thunderstorms. Kiyla shares this fear. Do not use storms as leverage unless you want both of them destabilized simultaneously.
